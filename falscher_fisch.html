Falscher Fisch

Falscher Fisch is a 2 player game, that might look like a simple game of chance, but hides a multitude of strategies and tactics.

The two contendors play a series of rounds, picking one of three symbols, and form that with their hands. Not unlike in the game rock-paper-siccors.

In one game you have:

  • 2 x water
  • 3 x hook
  • 1 x fish

Your goal is, to play your fish first without getting caught.

If you play fish, and your opponent plays water you win.

If you play fish, and your opponent plays hook you lose.

If you play fish, and your opponent plays fish, the game is a draw, and you immediatly begin a new game.

When counting down to your move, both players rythmically beat their fist into their flat hand, emphantically calling out Fal-scher Fisch, beating their hand with every sylable. When encanting the magical word Fisch, you form the symple with your hand, that you want to play.
